Clinical and Emergency Services
Either you need a checkup or having true medical emergency, society hospital provides 24/7 treatment services for you to stay healthy and fit.
Radiological Services
Radiological Services like X-rays and Ultrasounds are available to residents during work hours for quick diagnosis and treatment.
Pharmacy
Either you you need over the counter flu syrup or your insulin refilled, we have all the medicines available for our residents at very low cost and exceptional discounts 24/7.
Nursing Care
Society Hospital provides door step nursing care for minor treatments as well as assistance service for elderly patients. Our well trained staff is ready to take care of your daily medical needs just on your phone call.
